# Code of Conduct

This project is a small public knowledge repository. Contributors and
participants are expected to keep discussion respectful, specific, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and clear.
- Critique ideas and artifacts, not personal identity.
- Assume good intent when possible, but respect stated boundaries.
- Keep disagreements grounded in evidence, examples, or practical experience.
- Avoid publishing private information, secrets, or internal project material
  without permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated bad-faith disruption.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Submitting content that you do not have the right to publish.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block
participants who do not follow these expectations.

If you need to report a problem, open a private channel with the maintainer when
available. If no private channel is available, avoid posting sensitive details
publicly.
