# Lesson Inbox

This inbox collects candidate lessons before they become durable rules.

Do not treat every entry here as doctrine. This is a review queue.

## Intake Rule

Add a lesson here when all of these are true:

- it came from a real task, failure, review, or repeated cost;
- it is phrased as an operational rule, not a diary note;
- it may prevent repeated work or a repeated mistake;
- its final destination is not obvious enough to write directly into a durable
  rule file.

## Entry Template

```md
### YYYY-MM-DD - Short Title

- Source: [task, worker, command, review, or failure]
- Lesson: [what should future agents do differently?]
- Evidence: [what happened that supports this lesson?]
- Suggested destination: [skill, role brief, docs, test harness, or unknown]
- Intake status: candidate
- Review outcome: pending
```

## Review Outcomes

- `pending`: not reviewed yet.
- `promoted`: merged into a durable skill, role brief, or doc.
- `kept`: useful context, but not stable enough yet.
- `rejected`: too local, duplicated, wrong, stale, or unclear.
