# Example: Mature Role Brief

This is what a role brief looks like after several real tasks have run through
it.

Compare it to `templates/worker_roles/code-writer.md`.

The structure is the same. The difference is the **Local Knowledge** section at
the bottom. Those rules were not written on day one. They came from real
failures, were reviewed, and were promoted from the lesson inbox into the role
brief itself.

That section is the visible output of the refinement loop.
